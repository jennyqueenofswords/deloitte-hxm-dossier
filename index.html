The Framework: Hardwiring vs. Softwiring

Hardwiring

Formal elements: redesigned roles, accountability, decision rights, escalation protocols that dictate when work shifts from AI to human.

Softwiring

Informal elements: leadership behaviors, culture, psychological safety that give people trust and confidence to question, escalate, experiment with AI.

The Case Study to Know

A European telecom added an AI "expert" to customer service without changing workflows → 5% productivity lift.

Then they dedicated 90% of the rollout budget to redesigning human-AI interactions — new workflows, trust thresholds, escalation paths, training → 30% productivity increase.

This is the poster child for why "Humans x Machines" matters: tech alone gets you 5%. Intentional design gets you 30%.
